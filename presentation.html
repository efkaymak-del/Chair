Where we are

We are too often reactive.

Requests land on our desks with no notice. We scramble. The same data is rebuilt from scratch, again and again — pulling us away from teaching and research.

The scramble

The Rector's office asks; we drop everything to answer by Friday.

The duplication

Reports, statistics, activity lists — reassembled every single time.

The cost

Hours that should belong to our students and our scholarship.

The governing idea

Remove the friction that doesn't matter.
Protect the friction that does.

  • Frictionless capture. Information records itself, once, as a by-product of work we already do.
  • Deliberate approval. Decisions still go to Council. Minutes are still approved. Accountability is the friction worth keeping.
  • Proactive, not reactive. When the request comes, the answer is already there.

I'm not here to automate our judgment away. I'm here to give you back your time.

My commitment

Transparency. Full stop.

Every portfolio, every activity, every workload — visible to all of us. Not to measure anyone, but so that the load is shared fairly and no one's quiet work goes unseen.

This binds me too. My own duties, my own activity, go into the same open record. That is what makes it fair to ask it of everyone.

Building on what Council already decided

This makes our STAR & FIBAA work easier, not heavier.

Decision 9 already mandates a continuous-improvement evidence file built on the PUKÖ loop. The system I propose is simply the engine that makes that file fill itself.

  • Capture once → serves accreditation evidence, the Rector's office, and the workload record at the same time.
  • Every event, every committee meeting, every activity becomes PUKÖ evidence the moment it happens.
  • No more pre-visit scramble. The loop is always turning.
